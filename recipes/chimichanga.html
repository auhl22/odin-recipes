Chimichanga

Description

Add cooked chicken, refried beans, salsa, cheese and spices to a large mixing bowl and combine until smooth. Taste and adjust seasonings, if needed. Divide the filling between the tortillas, placing a big spoonful on top of each. Fold it tightly, pulling the sides in, like a burrito.

At this point you can choose to bake them, toast them in a skillet, or deep fry them. Each of those methods will allow them to get a little crispy on the outside, just like we want them.

Ingredients

  • 2c cooked chicken
  • 1 can refried beans
  • 1/2c salsa
  • 1t cumin
  • 1/2t chili powder
  • 1c shredded cheese
  • 2 green onions
  • 3T oil
  • 6 large flour tortillas
  1. Cook chicken in pan until tender and no longer pink, chop up
  2. Add refried beans, chicken, cheese, salsa, spices, and green onions to bowl and mix to combine
  3. Place about 1/2c of mixture in center of each tortilla
  4. Fold opposite sides over filling and rill like a burrito
  5. preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Brush chimichangas lightly with oil and bake for about 25 minutes
